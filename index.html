FREQUENTLY ASKED

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Will TEKRISE replace my tennis coach?
No — and it's not meant to. TEKRISE does one thing: it measures your swing and shows you what's happening. That's maybe 10–15% of what a great coach does — it can't read your opponent, run live drills, feed balls, fix your grip mid-swing, or keep you motivated. Think of it as the slow-motion replay and the measuring tape: the eye, not the coach.
I don't have a coach — is this still for me?
Absolutely. If you practise on your own, TEKRISE gives you the clear, technical feedback that's otherwise hard to get without someone watching — so you work on the right thing next instead of grooving the same mistake. Improve on your own schedule, at your own pace.
I'm a coach — how does TEKRISE help me?
It solves your biggest problem: students reinforcing bad habits in the six days between lessons. They arrive having practised the right things, so you spend less time repeating corrections and more on strategy, footwork and match play. A Coach dashboard shows what each student worked on, and you earn a share on every student you refer. See For Coaches →
Is the app really free?
Yes — the core swing analysis is free to use, and we'll always do our best to keep it that way. Running it isn't free for us (servers and AI cost money on every analysis), which is exactly why donations help. There may be optional paid extras down the line, but the feedback that helps you improve stays free.
Where does my donation go?
Straight into keeping TEKRISE alive and free: the server and AI costs behind every analysis, new features and shot types, and the time it takes to keep improving the app. We're an independent project with no outside investors — donations are what keep us going.
How accurate is the AI?
TEKRISE tracks your body pose frame-by-frame on-device and checks your racket using AI vision around the moment of impact. It's genuinely useful for spotting technique patterns and tracking change over time — but it's an estimate from a single phone camera, not a motion-capture lab. We're honest about that: it's a guide, not gospel, but it will keep getting better.
How do I support TEKRISE?
The easiest way is a one-off donation via Ko-fi — give whatever feels right, even a couple of dollars helps. You can also help by telling a hitting partner about the app, or sending us feedback so we can make it better. Questions? Email hello@tekrise.app.
